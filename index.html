Frequently Asked Questions

What is Disney+?

+

Disney+ is the destination for Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, National Geographic, Star, and more universes.

What can I watch on Disney+?

+

Disney+ offers a growing collection of stories:

  • Exclusive original series and films
  • Timeless classics from Walt Disney Animation Studios
  • Entertainment stories with comedies, dramas, true stories, etc.
  • National Geographic documentaries, as well as special productions and more.

What is the price of a Disney+ subscription?

+

You can choose from three subscription levels to Disney+:

  • Premium (11.99 €/month or 119.99 €/year): Up to 4K Ultra HD and HDR resolution. 4 simultaneous streams. Downloads for offline viewing on up to 10 devices. Dolby Atmos sound on compatible content.
  • Standard (8.99 €/month or 89.99 €/year): Up to 1080p Full HD resolution. 2 simultaneous streams. Downloads for offline viewing on up to 10 devices. 5.1 surround sound on compatible content.
  • Basic (5.99 €/month): Up to 1080p Full HD resolution. 2 simultaneous streams. 5.1 surround sound on compatible content. Ad-supported content.

What devices and platforms can I use with Disney+?

+

Disney+ is available on mobile devices, web browsers, game consoles, TV decoders, and connected TVs.

Does a Disney+ subscription include a commitment period?

+

Disney+ is commitment-free, and you can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation will take effect at the end of the paid period.

  1. Log in to www.DisneyPlus.com
  2. Select your profile
  3. Select Account
  4. Select your subscription
  5. Select Cancel Subscription
  6. Select Continue to Cancel
